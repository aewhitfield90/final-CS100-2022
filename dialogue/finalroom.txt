You proceed to the next room,

Looking around your mouth starts to water and your eyes go wide! A room filled with mountains of gold coins, enough to make Scrooge McDuck jealous. You rush into the room collecting a handful of gold pieces and shoving them into your pockets before you hear a voice from the other side of the room.

“Foolish mortal, you dare steal from the tomb of the great and powerful Skeleton King Nigel!?”

You pause for a moment before letting out a snicker.

“Who names their kid Nigel?” you think to yourself.

“Do not insult those who bestowed upon me my title! Soon you will not be able to think any thoughts at all, your blood will stain the coins you so hastily took from my collection!”

Nigel the Skeleton King stands up gripping his weapon, and charges straight for you.
